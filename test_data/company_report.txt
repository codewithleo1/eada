EADA Company Report 2024

Executive Summary
EADA Corp achieved record revenue of 450 million dollars in 2024, representing a 32% year-over-year growth. The Electronics division was the top performer contributing 60% of total revenue.

Division Performance
Electronics Division generated 270 million dollars in revenue with 600 units sold across laptops, phones, and monitors. The division launched 3 new product lines in Q3.

Furniture Division generated 75 million dollars in revenue with 240 units sold. Supply chain improvements reduced costs by 15%.

Apparel Division generated 60 million dollars in revenue with 550 units sold. International expansion drove 40% of division growth.

Key Highlights
- Launched new AI-powered inventory management system
- Expanded to 12 new international markets
- Employee headcount grew from 1200 to 1800
- Customer satisfaction score reached 4.8 out of 5

Outlook for 2025
Management projects 25% revenue growth targeting 562 million dollars. Key initiatives include expanding the Electronics division, launching a new Sports category, and investing in warehouse automation.
